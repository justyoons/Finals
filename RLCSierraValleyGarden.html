About Robinsons Land

Robinsons Land Corporation (RLC), one of the country’s leading real estate companies, develops and operates shopping malls, hotels, mixed-use properties, office buildings, residential condominiums, and housing projects nationwide.

Incorporated on June 4, 1980, RLC is the real estate arm of JG Summit Holdings, Inc., a major Philippine conglomerate involved in consumer foods, agro-industrial and commodity products, telecommunications, petrochemicals, aviation, and finance.

About Sierra Valley Gardens

Sierra Valley Gardens is a sprawling residential community within Sierra Valley — a master-planned, mixed-use development designed to nurture a thriving, independent community. Ideal for young professionals, start-up families, and investors, it features lifestyle destinations, business hubs, and residential spaces.

Mission:

Communities provided with opportunities for improved education, health & nutrition, community development, disaster response and sustainable environment.

Vision:

A better quality of life for Filipino communities.
